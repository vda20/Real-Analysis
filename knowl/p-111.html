Suppose that \(x\lt y\text{.}\)
\(x+((-x) +(-y)) \lt y+((-x) +(-y))\) By Axiom O3
\((x+(-x)) +(-y) \lt y+((-y) +(-x))\) By Axioms A1 and A2
\(0 +(-y) \lt (y+(-y)) +(-x)\) By Axioms A4 and A2
\(-y \lt 0 +(-x)\) By Axioms A3 and A4
\(-y \lt -x\) By Axiom A3
The implication \(-y\lt -x\Rightarrow x\lt y\) is similar.
